Maryland: Clean Slate

RBIJ is currently engaging local business leaders in Maryland around the state’s ongoing Clean Slate effort. To date, this includes:

  • Business sign-on letter
  • Private advocacy to key legislators
  • Public testimony on behalf of nine businesses
  • Speaking opportunities: University of Baltimore School of Law
  • Op-ed: Flagger Force

Stats

1 million Marylanders — 22% of the state’s adult population — have some form of arrest or conviction record.

More than 400,000 individuals are eligible to have their record sealed right now.

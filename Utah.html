Utah: Clean Slate

RBIJ mobilized businesses — and their leaders — to publicly and privately support legislation relating to Clean Slate by:

  • Business video series (incl. Intermountain Healthcare)
  • Press conference with remarks from the CEO of the Salt Lake Chamber of Commerce
  • Collaboration with the Utah Jazz, including social posts from an influential player
  • Educated 100+ companies, Chambers, and associations on the bill’s impact
  • Op-eds and media: Jeff Korzenik (Fifth Third), Nyra Jordan (AmFam), FT Moral Money, Marketplace

Clean Slate was signed into law by Governor Gary Herbert in 2019 and implementation began in February 2022.

Current Activity

Utah: Clean Slate

RBIJ mobilized awareness and support for Utah’s Clean Slate through education, media, and partnerships. To date, this includes:

  • Business video series, including one with Intermountain Healthcare, Utah's largest employer
  • Press conference with remarks from the CEO of the Salt Lake Chamber of Commerce
  • Collaboration with the NBA team, Utah Jazz, including social media posts from an influential player
  • Educated more than 100 companies, Chambers, and business associations across Utah on the bill’s impact
  • Op-eds and media: Jeff Korzenik (Fifth Third Bank), Nyra Jordan (American Family Insurance), Financial Times Moral Money, Marketplace

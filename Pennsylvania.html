Pennsylvania: Clean Slate 3.0

RBIJ mobilized businesses — and their leaders — to publicly and privately support legislation relating to Clean Slate by:

  • Business sign-on letter
  • Testimony from business voices, including the Pennsylvania Chamber of Business & Industry, in support
  • Private advocacy to key legislators
  • Op-eds: Gabriel Mandujano (Wash Cycle Laundry), Mike Doner (Flagger Force), Alex Halper (PA Chamber), Abbey Carlton (Indeed)

In December 2023, Governor Josh Shapiro signed PA's Clean Slate expansion bill into law, decreasing waiting periods and expanding eligibility to include individuals with qualifying felonies.

Stats

2.8 million Pennsylvanians — 28% of the state’s adult population — have some form of arrest or conviction record.

More than a million Pennsylvanians have had their records sealed to date.

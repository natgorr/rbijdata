Current Activity

Kentucky — Clean Slate

RBIJ is currently engaging local business leaders in Kentucky around the state’s ongoing Clean Slate effort. To date, this includes:

  • Business sign-on letter
  • Private advocacy to key legislators
  • Public testimony on behalf of businesses
  • Speaking opportunities: Chambers Advancing Justice for their Workforces (Kentucky Chamber and Kentucky Chamber Foundation)

Stats

1.3 million Kentuckians — 38% of the state’s adult population — have some form of arrest or conviction record.

If the commonwealth were a country, it would rank seventh highest in the world for its rate of incarceration.

At the same time, Kentucky businesses are currently trying to fill more than 160,000 open jobs.

Connecticut: Clean Slate (implementation)

RBIJ mobilized businesses — and their leaders — to publicly and privately support Clean Slate legislation and its implementation by:

  • Private advocacy from 15 influential businesses
  • Business toolkit
  • 3BL's Network Effect Event
  • Op-ed: by JPMorgan Chase’s Monica Nation & State Senator Gary Winfield

In 2021, Governor Lamont signed Connecticut’s Clean Slate legislation into law. In 2023–2024, RBIJ helped raise awareness around the implementation of the law and its benefits.

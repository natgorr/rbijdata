New York: Clean Slate

RBIJ mobilized businesses — and their leaders — to publicly and privately support Clean Slate legislation and its implementation in New York by:

  • Sign-on letter
  • Private advocacy to key legislators
  • Media & Op-eds: Mike Novogratz, Founder and CEO of Galaxy Digital
  • Media: The Independent
  • Speaking opportunities: Bloomberg’s Equality Summit
  • Blogs: ASBN
  • Blog: Greyston Bakery

In 2023, Governor Hochul signed New York’s Clean Slate legislation into law, providing relief to millions of New Yorkers.

Stats

3.7 million New Yorkers — 24% of the state’s adult population — have some form of arrest or conviction record.

New York loses approximately $12.6 billion in lost wages every year due to reduced earnings related to a conviction record.

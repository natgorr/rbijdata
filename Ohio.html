Ohio: Clean Slate

RBIJ is currently engaging local business leaders in Ohio around the state’s ongoing Clean Slate effort. To date, this includes:

  • Private advocacy to key legislators
  • Public testimony on behalf of businesses
  • Op-ed with Phil Petrill (CEO of Hot Chicken Takeover) as byline in the Columbus Dispatch

Past Wins

Colorado: Clean Slate 2.0

RBIJ mobilized businesses — and their leaders — to publicly and privately support legislation relating to Clean Slate by:

  • Sign-on statement with more than 30 business leaders
  • Public testimony from six business leaders
  • Op-eds: Bryant Dulin, CEO of KOBEYO
  • Op-eds: Chris Romer, President and CEO of Vail Valley Partnership
  • Op-eds: Drew Patterson, CEO of Basic Industries

In 2022, the Colorado General Assembly passed the Colorado Clean Slate Act with nearly unanimous support. In 2024, a bill was signed into law that would ensure the successful implementation of the Colorado Clean Slate Act.

Colorado: Occupational Licensing

RBIJ mobilized businesses — and their leaders — to publicly and privately support legislation that expands access to occupational licensing in Colorado by:

  • Sign-on statement with over 10 businesses
  • Written testimony by five influential and local chambers and businesses
  • Op-ed: James Repenning, Founder and CEO of R&R Head Labs
  • Blog: Eric Miller, Owner of Labor Depot, LLC
  • Small Business Majority polling amplification

In 2024, HB24-1004 was signed into law, ensuring fairer access to licensed jobs, reduced recidivism, and an expansion of Colorado’s economy.

Stats

1.4 million Coloradans — 31% of the state’s adult population — have some form of arrest or conviction record.

In Colorado, nearly one in every six jobs requires an occupational license.

Colorado has nearly 200,000 open jobs – with only 100,000 workers to fill them.
